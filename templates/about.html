About the project

A field notebook, automated

OptiCrop is a Machine Learning-based web application that turns soil and climate readings — Nitrogen (N), Phosphorous (P), Potassium (K), temperature, humidity, pH, and rainfall into an instant crop recommendation. It's built for farmers choosing what to plant, researchers studying crop-environment interactions, and policymakers shaping evidence based agricultural strategy.
